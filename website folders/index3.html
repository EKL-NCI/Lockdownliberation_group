Minigame

Another study tip is to create flash cards!

a flash card is a small card with a little bit of information, say a important date if its history, or the name of a weather process if its geography etc

you would create a pile of these flashcards, and then shuffle them and go through one by one, either saying out loud or writing down the associated information

this helps you study as the random order of the pile of flash cards would prevent you from being able to memorize which card comes next, forcing you to think on the fly

aswell as this though, the repitition of the information itself will cause it to become engrained in your memory!

in the spirit of randomization, i made a quick little guessing game, where you get shown 2 numbers and have to guess what they are added together!

studying is important, but remember to have fun!


good luck!
